FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide same day service?

Yes! If you book before 1 PM, our technician will visit the same day. We cover all areas in Navi Mumbai including Vashi, Kharghar, Nerul, Belapur, Airoli, and more.

What is your service warranty?

All our repairs come with a 30-day service warranty. If the same problem recurs within 30 days, we will fix it at no extra charge.

Do you use genuine spare parts?

Yes, we use only original or OEM-quality spare parts. Parts are billed separately at actual cost with a receipt provided.

How do I book a service?

You can book online through our booking form, call us directly, or send us a message on WhatsApp. We'll confirm your appointment within 30 minutes.

What areas do you cover in Navi Mumbai?

We cover all of Navi Mumbai including Vashi, Kharghar, Nerul, Belapur, Seawoods, Panvel, Airoli, Ghansoli, Kopar Khairane, and surrounding areas.
